PRESIDENTIAL SUITE

Description

The Presidential Suite is tiled in flawless, white Thassos marble, defined by classic white columns and ornamented with a black piano and a life size bronze statue. In the dining room, a brass telescope looks out over the ocean, while from the living room, the Queen's Necklace can be seen in all its glory. Spread over 2,056 square feet (191 square metres), the Presidential Suite includes a master bedroom and a living space, a separate living room and a dining room with ocean views, a work space, a powder room, a pantry and a separate entrance for the private butler. The suite is furnished with luxurious gold upholstery, throughout. Entered through an annex that houses a personal fitness machine, the bathroom is immaculate with white marble and sparkling spotlights. Features include a full length bathtub with a hand held chrome shower, a separate vari pressure shower, an electronic WC and a 17" waterproof television.
