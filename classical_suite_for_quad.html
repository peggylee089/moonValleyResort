CLASSICAL SUITE FOR QUAD

Description

The Classic Suites at Moon Valley Resort are the quintessence of Roman living and many feature magnificent panoramic city views. Relax and unwind in the sumptuously furnished living room or enjoy the space and seclusion of the separate bedroom. These one bedroom suites are elegantly decorated in soothing tones and impeccably furnished with attention to detail, offering a residential feel with exceptionally high ceilings and tall windows. The foyer entrance guides you into the master bedroom or the spacious living area, complete with desk. Pamper yourself in the stylish new bathrooms decorated in white and gold hues, with exquisite mosaics, marble vanities, a walk-in rain head shower, separate bath and luxurious Italian Bottega Veneta toiletries.
