CHALET FOR DOUBLE

Description

The Moon Valley Resort Chalet is a luxury 5 star chalet located in the heart of Taipei, with views overlooking the village and of the delightful ocean. Situated in one of the world's most desirable locations, this is a unique opportunity to rent an exceptional chalet, with award-winning staff and service for the holiday of a lifetime. Marking a fine presence on the shores of our private peninsula, our chalet series are known for their unparalleled panoramic ocean vistas. With lavish spaces furnished to provide a stay beyond comfort and indulgence, coupled with spectacular 180-degrees views of the sparkling Pacific Ocean, escape to our holiday resort in Taipei luxuriate in total bliss. Formed by two inter-connecting buildings, our chalet is perfect for families who enjoy spending quality time together. Spread out in the beautifully appointed spaces with room to spare while enjoying all-natural surroundings.
