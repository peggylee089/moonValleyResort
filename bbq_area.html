BBQ AREA

Description

The Moon Valley Resort BBQ Area is set in beautiful park like grounds, leafy mature trees and a meandering stream. A light, airy holiday atmosphere encourages a great time, whether it is around the barbecue with friends and family, or with the children feeding the eels at the end of the jetty. We have two barbeque areas for those staying in the park. The first is beside the main facilities block in the centre of the park. A new complex, with 2 free modern gas bbqs, a large open fire is completed with an out-door kitchen. This area is adjacent to the park playground and new swimming pool complex. The second is by our Motel units. Moon Valley Resort provide places where you and your families can have a BBQ on your own. We will have performance every evening. Come and join us and have a great time.
